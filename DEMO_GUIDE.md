# 🎯 Merchant Twin Simulation - Demo Guide

## Quick Start for Judges/Demos

### 1. Start the Application
```bash
# Terminal 1: Start backend
cd backend
npm start

# Terminal 2: Start frontend
cd frontend
npm start
```

Access at: `http://localhost:3001`

---

## 🎬 Demo Script (5 Minutes)

### Act 1: Introduction (30 seconds)
**Landing on Dashboard**

"Welcome to the Merchant Twin Simulation Platform - an AI-powered decision intelligence console that helps optimize merchant onboarding flows."

**Point out the flow diagram:**
- CSV Upload → AI Merchants → Onboarding Portal → Insights

"We use synthetic AI merchants to simulate real-world onboarding scenarios, detecting friction points before they impact real users."

---

### Act 2: Setup Simulation (1 minute)
**Click "Start Simulation"**

"Let me show you how easy it is to run a simulation..."

**Step through the guided workflow:**

1. **Upload Merchant CSV** - "We load merchant profiles with varying business types"
2. **Upload Network Metrics** - "Different network conditions from 4G to 2G"
3. **Upload Bio/Persona CSV** - "Merchant personas with different digital literacy levels"
   - **Show validation**: "The system detected 3 merchant personas and 4 network profiles"
4. **Select Channel** - "Choose Web, USSD, or Mobile App" (select Web)
5. **Configure Portal** - "Point to your onboarding portal URL"
6. **Review & Run** - "Review configuration and launch"

**Click "Start Simulation"**

---

### Act 3: Live Monitoring (2 minutes)
**Auto-navigates to Live Insights**

"Now watch as our AI merchants navigate the onboarding flow in real-time..."

**Point to the top metrics:**
- "We're simulating 5 merchants"
- "Current completion rate is 65%"
- "Drop-off rate of 35% - let's see why"

**Point to the Timeline:**
- "Here's the live event stream - each card shows a merchant's journey"
- "Green events are successes"
- "Yellow warnings indicate friction"
- "Red errors show failures"

**Highlight specific events:**
- "Merchant #14 just loaded the portal"
- "Now filling business details"
- "Uh oh - network delay detected on 2G connection"
- "The merchant is retrying..."

**Point to AI Assistant Panel:**
- "Our AI continuously analyzes the simulation"
- "It's detecting that Step 3 causes 42% drop-off"
- "Low-end device personas struggle with document upload"
- "The AI recommends simplifying the upload flow"

### Act 4: Insights & Value (1.5 minutes)

"The platform automatically identifies friction points:"

**Point to friction detection:**

**Point to recommendations:**

- "Implement offline support"
