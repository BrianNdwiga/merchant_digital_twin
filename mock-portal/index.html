Lipa Na M-PESA Products

Choose the right product for your business needs. Both options allow you to start accepting cashless payments immediately.

Most Popular

M-PESA Business Till
(Buy Goods Till)

Enables business owners to collect payments on the till and use the money collected to make other transactions directly from the till.

  • Pay to another Buy Goods Till
  • Pay to a mobile number
  • Pay to utilities (Paybill) till
  • Withdraw cash at an M-PESA agent
  • Sell airtime and earn commission

Ideal for retail, supermarkets, fuel stations, restaurants, salons.

Fundraising

Short-Term Paybill

A product that allows for fundraising of funds for different needs including medical bills, funerals, weddings, education, and charity or catastrophes.

  • Medical bill fundraising
  • Wedding and funeral fund collection
  • Education fund management
  • Charity and catastrophe support
  • Fund, Funeral Fund, Education Fund

Ideal for NGOs, community groups, social events and charitable causes.

Individual Till (Buy Goods)

Seamless funds collection and transaction from your till

0.55% per transaction
Features
Collect payments from businesses and customers on the till
Make payments directly from your till — Pay Bill or Till
Make payments to customers
Access via USSD *234*2# or M-Pesa Business App
Real-time, full statements
Withdraw funds to nominated number or at M-PESA Agent
Requirements
Application Form — completed online
ID Documents — National ID, Passport, Alien ID, or Military ID
Settlement Options
Individual
Settle to owner's M-PESA
Individual
Settle to owner's Bank Account
Rates and Charges

0.55% charge, not more than KShs 200 per transaction for money collected on the till.

Collections of Ksh 200 and below are FREE.

No customer charges for payments made using Lipa na M-PESA Buy Goods, except for payments made at fuel stations.

Short-Term Paybill

Fundraising and short-term fund collection made easy

Fundraising Product
Use Cases
Medical bill fundraising campaigns
Wedding and funeral fund collection
Education fund management
Charity and catastrophe support
Requirements
Application Form — completed online
ID Documents — National ID, Passport, Alien ID, or Military ID
Fund Description — purpose and target amount
Rates and Charges

Standard M-PESA transaction charges apply based on amount sent.

No additional charges for setting up a Short-Term Paybill account.

Account valid for up to 90 days — renewable on request.
